Upgrade Your Space with Hometex's 10-Pack Sublimation Pillow Set

Elevate your interior design with a collection of 16" x 16" throw pillow inserts and matching sublimation covers. These premium-quality pillows are crafted from 100% polyester fabric, offering durability and seamless compatibility with sublimation ink. The 385 GSM fabric provides a refined linen texture, bringing a touch of sophistication to your decor while ensuring vibrant, long-lasting prints.

Each pillow has a hidden zipper for effortless insertion and removal, allowing for easy maintenance and reuse. The inserts are filled with high-loft polyester fibers to deliver superior comfort and support, creating a soft, plump feel without losing their shape. Plus, they are machine washable and dryer safe, making them a practical and stylish addition to any home or commercial setting.

Why Choose This Pillow Set?

Whether you're transforming your living room for a cozy update or customizing pillows for a boutique or retail space, this set is designed to meet both personal and professional needs. The polyester fabric is highly elastic, preventing shrinking and ensuring a consistent look over time. It also holds up well under heat press printing, with reliable ink bonding that enhances the quality of your design.

These pillows are also perfect for use with Heat Transfer Vinyl, supporting lower temperatures and shorter press times for efficient, high-quality results. Their modern aesthetic and functional design make them a versatile and essential choice for those who value both style and performance in home decor.
